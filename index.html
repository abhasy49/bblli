OUR SERVICES

Physical Security

We  help the police intellectually  in the protection of personnel, hardware, software, networks and data from physical actions that could cause serious loss or damage to an enterprise, agency, institution due to theft,terrorism etc.

Environmental Security

We examine threats posed by environmental events and trends to individuals and communities and possible ways to avoid or sanitize affected  area through practical demonstrations,Hand bills etc

Food Security

Through media intervention we create   awareness to the Government and NGOs on the need for people to have  access to sufficient safe and nutritious food that meet their dietary needs and food preferences for an active and healthy life.

Social Security

We have action program to promote the welfare of members through pension and health insurance measures guaranteeing access to monetery facility to empower and promote  well-being to members
